Purpose

DetectCore provides a common, extensible framework for radar detection evaluation that can be reused across multiple radar programs and platforms.

Why is it needed?

* Standardize radar detection logic across projects
* Reduce duplicated detection algorithms
* Improve maintainability through modular components
* Support rapid integration of new radar capabilities
* Enable reusable feature extraction and evaluation logic
